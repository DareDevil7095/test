HOW TO DO LAUNDRY

General Laundry Rules

  • Follow detergent instructions - use recommended amounts.
  • Check fabric care labels - symbols contain important wash information.
  • Sort laundry by color and soil level.
  • Treat stains immediately.
  • Use proper wash cycles and clean the dryer lint trap frequently.

Sorting It Out

Sorting helps prevent color bleeding, fading, and garment damage. It groups clothing with similar wash temperatures, detergent needs, and cycle types.

• Wash whites separately.
• Combine pastels and medium shades.
• Wash brights and darks separately.

Light synthetic fabrics easily absorb darker dyes, so keep them apart.

• Separate heavily soiled items from lightly worn clothing.
• Soil can redeposit during washing, causing whites to appear gray.
• Keeping heavily used garments apart protects lighter fabrics.

• The Unmatched Set: Mix small + large items for better drum movement.
• The Fabric Types: Separate delicates and knits for gentle cycles.
• The Lint Losers: Wash lint-producing items (Fuzzy sweat shirts, chenille robes, flannels) separately.
• The Fluorescents: Wash fluorescents alone; avoid strong stain removers unless tested.

Removing Stains

Take care to wash away any spots and stains on your washables. It helps to know exactly what type of stain it is for your best chance at removal.

• Identify the stain type before treatment.
• Treat stains immediately - heat sets them permanently.
• Pretreat using stain remover, liquid detergent, or paste.
• Test stain remover on an inside seam first.
• Blot stains instead of rubbing.

Light synthetic fabrics easily absorb darker dyes, so keep them apart.

• Sugary drinks may dry invisible—always treat them.
• Apply bleach to the entire garment to avoid uneven patches.
• Enzyme pretreatments can help remove older stains.
• Always wash the full garment after pretreating.

GUIDE TO FABRIC CARE SYMBOLS

Understand the symbols on clothing labels to wash and care for garments correctly.

From washing temperatures to ironing instructions, reading the symbols helps protect your fabrics.

Washing

Check water temperature and machine settings recommended on the label.

Drying

Follow tumble-dry or air-dry instructions to avoid shrinking or damage.

Ironing & Bleaching

Icons indicate safe ironing temperature and whether bleaching is allowed.
